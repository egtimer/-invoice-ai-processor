Frequently asked questions

What file formats do you support? ▼

We support PDF, PNG, JPG, JPEG, TIFF, and DOCX files. This includes scanned documents and photos taken with your phone.

How accurate is the extraction? ▼

With Claude AI enabled, we achieve up to 95% accuracy on standard invoices. The local-only mode achieves around 80% accuracy. Complex or handwritten invoices may have lower accuracy.

Is my data secure? ▼

Yes, we take security seriously. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't store your invoices permanently - they are deleted after processing unless you opt to save them.

Can I integrate with my accounting software? ▼

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include API access. You can integrate with QuickBooks, Xero, SAP, Odoo, and any other system that accepts JSON data.

Do you offer a free trial? ▼

Yes! Our Free plan lets you process 50 invoices per month at no cost. You can also try the Pro plan free for 14 days with no credit card required.
